Discord Server

Jet's Dream World is a discord server where people hang out and chill. We have 500 emojis, an economy system, awesome people and much more.

Economy System

Our economy system allows you to gain money by chatting and being unmuted in voice. Every few minutes or so, an event happens in general, like unscramble a word. If you do it first, you get coins. When you have enough coins, you can buy roles and custom vc's.

Community

We have a ton of active and awesome community members! Our staff are really nice and so are our members. With over 400k members, there are people making new friends 24/7.
